What is Agrow-help ?


AGROW help was designed with an idea to make the art of farming easy and accessible to all. We have designed the application to be useful to people who want to take up farming (We call them as beginners) and for the people who have experience in farming (we call them experienced farmers).

Beginner farmers (like us) are those who are willing to venture into the field of farming. He can choose from a list of crops and he can all the inputs given by the experienced farmers on that crop. He can choose the best input based on the number of likes each input has received. The forum works in the same manner as that of the beginners farmers. The beginners are also provided with the facility of renting machinery.

Experienced farmers are those individuals who have prior knowledge in this field. We have planned on making use of this knowledge for the benefit of the community. The experienced farmer can choose from a variety of crops and give his input pertaining to various issues such as the appropriate time for the crop, the fertilizers used ,etc. This knowledge can then be retrieved by the beginner farmer at any point of time. We believe that information is wealth. This has propelled us to create an application-centric forum for querying cum interaction. The experienced farmer can answer the questions posted on the forum and can also post a question if he has any. We have an interactive system for renting tools and other machinery. The interface is easy to use and can be used by any layman without any hassle.


Our Philosophy

It’s a known fact that we would be needing framers multiple times a day starting from our breakfast to dinner. We have decided to come up with an application to help these farmers so that everyone gets to eat without any shortage.
